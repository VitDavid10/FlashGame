Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

At PillWars (pillwars.fun) we take your privacy seriously and collect the minimum data possible to make the game work and keep it fair. This page explains, in plain language, what data we handle and why.

What we collect

  • Your IP address, anonymized. As soon as you connect we trim your IP (we drop the last block, e.g. 178.105.243.x). We never store or display your full IP.
  • The nickname you choose when playing (you decide it; we don't ask for your real name).
  • Your approximate country, derived from the anonymized IP, for statistics only.
  • Game stats: matches played, kills, deaths.
  • Website usage data via Google Analytics (pages visited, browser, etc.).
  • Contact form: if you write to us, the name, email and message you submit (handled by Web3Forms).

Why we use it

  • To run the online game (connecting you to matches).
  • Security and moderation: detecting abuse and removing cheaters or disruptive players.
  • Anonymous statistics to understand usage and improve the game.

The legal basis is our legitimate interest in providing a secure, working service.

How long we keep it

Connection and moderation logs are deleted automatically after 60 days. Game stats are kept while the game stays active.

Who we share it with

We do not sell or trade your data. We use a few external services that may process technical data:

  • Google Analytics (website statistics).
  • ip-api.com, only to derive the country from the anonymized IP.
  • Web3Forms, only to deliver contact-form messages to us.

Your rights

You can request access to your data or its deletion by writing to privacidad@pillwars.fun. As the data is anonymized, in many cases we won't be able to link it to you, but we'll handle any reasonable request.

Cookies

The game itself uses no tracking cookies. The website may use Google Analytics cookies. You can block them in your browser and the game will still work.
